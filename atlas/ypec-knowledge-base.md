## Frequently Asked Questions

### For Potential Clients

**Q: How are your chefs vetted?**
A: All chefs undergo background checks, reference verification, and skills assessment. We only work with experienced professionals who have worked in fine dining or private service.

**Q: Can I meet the chef before committing?**
A: Absolutely. We arrange a meet & greet (virtual or in-person) and can schedule a trial meal for an additional fee.

**Q: What if we're not satisfied with our chef?**
A: We offer chef reassignment if the match isn't working. Your satisfaction is our priority.

**Q: Do you provide ingredients?**
A: Chefs handle all grocery shopping and ingredient sourcing, billed separately or included in your monthly service fee depending on your agreement.

**Q: What equipment do we need?**
A: Most well-appointed kitchens have everything needed. Your chef can provide recommendations for any specialty equipment.

**Q: Can our chef accommodate dietary restrictions?**
A: Yes. All our chefs have experience with various dietary needs (gluten-free, dairy-free, vegan, keto, etc.).

**Q: How far in advance do we need to book for events?**
A: For special events, we recommend 2-4 weeks notice to ensure chef availability and proper planning.

**Q: Is there a minimum commitment?**
A: No long-term contracts required. We offer month-to-month agreements, though 6 or 12-month commitments receive priority scheduling.

### For Potential Chefs

**Q: How do you find clients for us?**
A: Through our website, referrals, partnerships, and marketing efforts. We handle all lead generation.

**Q: What's the revenue split?**
A: Chefs receive 60-70% of the hourly rate, depending on experience and household commitment level.

**Q: How many households will I serve?**
A: Typically 1-5 depending on your preference and each household's needs. Full-time placements are 1-2 households.

**Q: Do I need my own equipment?**
A: No. You'll use the household's kitchen equipment. We can recommend items if needed.

**Q: What if a client cancels?**
A: We have cancellation policies in place. Regular clients provide 48-hour notice or pay for scheduled time.

**Q: Can I suggest menu changes?**
A: Absolutely. You collaborate with households on menus that suit their preferences and your expertise.
